Our Purpose

About Us

We aim to promote cybersecurity, conduct group learning, explore various technologies, network with other students, and generate valuable experience through practical labs.

Our Plan

We plan on using TryHackMe and Hack The Box as a tool to gain practical hands-on experience. While using these platforms to learn we will strive to enter a hacking competition as a university team.

How To Join

Register for the club on Symplicity.

The YSU Cybersecurity Club operates remotely, there are no in person meetings. Group meetings and discussions are conducted in Discord. Once you join the Discord server, you will be required to complete two forms of verification and an introduction to gain access. You will not be granted access to the entire server until all requirements listed have been met.

If you are having trouble signing up for the club, please reach out to the club president or faculty advisor.
